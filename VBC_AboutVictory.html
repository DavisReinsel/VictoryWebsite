Meet our Pastor

Victory Baptist Church of Maple Grove is a Bible believing and preaching church.

Our Pastor, Lawrence Maupin, prepares and preaches two messages each Sunday and one message for our Wednesday night Bible Study and prayer meeting. Preaching from the King James Version, he believes strongly in expositional preaching, going through the Bible verse by verse, explaining the history and culture behind the verse and often going back to the original Greek to expand on the meaning. This method of study and preaching infuses his messages with a depth and richness that enriches the congregation’s understanding of God’s Holy Word.
